Wondise Halloween Tea Lights - Spooky and Safe Indoor Lighting

These Halloween Tea Lights are a delightful way to bring some eerie charm to your home, perfect for the spooky season. Each candle is adorned with a striking spider web decal, casting a mysterious glow that enhances your Halloween decor. The flameless LED technology ensures they are both safe and clean, with no smoke, wax, or drips to worry about. Ideal for small spaces and easy to place on tables, windows, or fireplaces, these 1.5-inch diameter tea lights are a great value with a 12-pack option.

Whether you're setting the mood for Halloween, fall celebrations, or a cozy indoor gathering, these LED tea lights are a must-have. Featuring a built-in 6-hour timer and an 18-hour off cycle, they automate their lighting, eliminating the need for constant supervision. Each candle is powered by a long-lasting battery, offering over 250 hours of continuous use. The included CR2032 battery enhances convenience, making these a reliable and stylish choice for any spooky setting.
