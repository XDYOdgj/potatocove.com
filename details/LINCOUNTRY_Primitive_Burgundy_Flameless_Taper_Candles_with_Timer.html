LINCOUNTRY Primitive Burgundy Flameless Taper Candles with Timer

Bring a touch of elegance and warmth to your home without the risks of open flames. These flameless taper candles feature a realistic 3D flickering wick, creating a soft, romantic glow that enhances any room. The primitive design, hand-dipped in rich burgundy tones, adds a rustic and timeless aesthetic to your decor.

With a built-in timer, these candles can be set to operate for 6 hours and then automatically shut off, making them safe and easy to use for families and pets. Whether you're preparing for a cozy dinner, a special celebration, or a festive holiday, these candles are the perfect addition to your table centerpiece, window decor, or wall display.

Battery operated and compact in size (6.75 inches tall), they are ideal for small spaces and versatile enough to be placed on shelves, counters, or mantles. These candles are not only a great gift option for any occasion but also a stylish and safe choice that brings charm and atmosphere to your home while ensuring peace of mind.

Key Features

  • Real wax construction for a natural look and feel
  • Battery operated with a programmable timer for 6 hours on and 18 hours off
  • 3D flickering wick for an authentic flame effect
  • Handcrafted design for a rustic and primitive aesthetic
  • Safe for use around kids, pets, and all environments

Why Choose These Candles?

These flameless taper candles are designed with both style and safety in mind. Perfect for weddings, birthdays, and holidays, they create a warm, inviting atmosphere that enhances your celebrations. The realistic flickering effect ensures that the ambiance is just as captivating as traditional candles, without the drawbacks of fire or heat.

Whether you're decorating your living room, adding a touch of holiday cheer, or looking for an elegant wall accent, these candles are the perfect solution. Their compact size and simple design make them ideal for small spaces, while the built-in timer adds convenience and peace of mind for everyday use.
