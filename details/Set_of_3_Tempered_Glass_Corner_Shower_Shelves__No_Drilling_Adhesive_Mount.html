Set of 3 Tempered Glass Corner Shower Shelves

These corner shelves combine durability, style, and convenience for your bathroom. Made from high-quality 8mm tempered glass, they are built to last and offer a safe, modern storage solution.

Durable & Safe Construction

Crafted with extra-thick 8mm tempered glass, these shelves are tough and resistant to breakage. In case of a break, the glass fragments remain blunt, reducing the risk of injury and ensuring safety for you and your family.

The rustproof zinc alloy brackets are designed to support up to 20 pounds, making them perfect for holding your daily bathroom essentials like soap, shampoo, and conditioners.

Space-Saving & Versatile Design

With a clever corner design, these shelves help maximize limited space in your bathroom or other areas of your home. They are ideal for those seeking a neat and organized way to display or store items of all sizes.

Use them in your bathroom, living room, kitchen, or bedroom to keep your items neat, accessible, and stylishly arranged.

Easy Installation & Maintenance

No drilling is required to install these shelves, which makes them perfect for renters or homeowners who prefer a hassle-free setup. They adhere securely to tile, glass, and marble surfaces and are easy to clean and maintain.

Perfect for Every Home

Whether you're looking to organize bathroom supplies or showcase decorative items in your living room, these shelves offer a versatile and eye-catching solution. Their modern appearance adds a touch of elegance to any space.
