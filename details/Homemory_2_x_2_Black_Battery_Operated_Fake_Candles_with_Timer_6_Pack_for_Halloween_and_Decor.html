Elegant Flameless Candles for Safe, Timeless Illumination

The Homemory 2" x 2" black flameless candles add a charming, warm glow to any setting without the risks of real fire. Their realistic LED lighting creates a lifelike candle experience, making them a perfect addition to your home decor. Whether you're preparing for a Halloween display, decorating pumpkins, or enhancing seasonal festivities, these candles are a safe and hassle-free alternative to traditional lighting solutions.

Each candle is powered by a single CR2450 coin battery, included for convenience, ensuring long-lasting use and simple replacement. The built-in timer automatically cycles the light on for 6 hours and off for 18 hours, eliminating the need for constant manual control. This feature makes them ideal for creating a cozy, inviting ambiance for any event, from weddings and parties to Christmas window arrangements and more.

With their versatile design, these battery-operated fake candles are suitable for both indoor and outdoor use, enhancing your Halloween decorations or any festive setup. They're also eco-friendly, offering a sustainable way to enjoy the perfect atmosphere without the worry of maintenance or fire hazards.
