Bring Magic to Your Holiday Decor with Yeelida's 12-Pack LED Taper Candles

These flameless, LED taper candles are the perfect way to create a warm, inviting atmosphere for your Christmas celebrations. Each candle features a crystal ball hanging from a gold hook, adding a touch of elegance and whimsy to your festive setup. Whether you're stringing them along a mantel, around a tree, or decorating your windows, these candles deliver a stunning and enchanting glow that captures the holiday spirit.

The battery operated design eliminates the need for messy wiring, making installation a breeze. With up to 150 hours of runtime using just one AAA battery, they stay lit all season long, offering hassle-free illumination and lasting value. Plus, the included remote control lets you easily adjust brightness and lighting modes, giving you full control over the ambiance. You can even set the timing to match the rhythm of your holiday activities, from twinkling to steady light.

Designed with safety and convenience in mind, these LED taper candles are ideal for families of all ages. The remote control has an extended range of 25 feet, allowing you to manage all candles from a distance. This makes them perfect for easy setup and enjoyment, encouraging everyone to join in the holiday fun. Complete with 12 candles, crystal ball hooks, a remote, and a user manual, this set offers everything you need to create a magical holiday display.
