DRomance Flameless Taper Candles - Elegance Meets Safety

Elevate your holiday decor with these beautifully designed DRomance Flameless Taper Candles. Perfect for those who love the ambiance of candlelight without the dangers of open flames, they bring a warm and inviting glow to any space. The real wax construction gives them a lifelike appearance, while the 3D wick design creates a realistic flickering effect that mimics the charm of traditional candles. These flameless candles are safe, easy to use, and ideal for indoor holiday displays, such as Christmas trees or Thanksgiving tables.

Equipped with a 10-key remote control, you can effortlessly adjust the brightness, turn them on and off, and set a cycle timer to suit your needs. Choose from 2, 4, 6, or 8 hours of illumination, and the timer will automatically repeat every 24 hours for maximum convenience. Their compact size, 0.79 x 9.9 inches, makes them perfect for small spaces and versatile for all holiday decorating needs. These remote control candles are a modern, safe, and stylish choice for anyone looking to enhance their festive atmosphere.
