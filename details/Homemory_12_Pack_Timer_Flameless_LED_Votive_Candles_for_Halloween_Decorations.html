Homemory 12 Pack Timer Flameless LED Votive Candles

These flameless votive candles from Homemory combine convenience and elegance. Built with a smart timer, they automatically cycle on for 6 hours and off for 18 hours, freeing you from daily manual tasks. Equipped with a long battery life of over 150 hours, they deliver consistent illumination without the need for frequent replacements. Crafted with a realistic wax-like plastic, they offer the same soft aesthetic as traditional candles but without the risks. Each candle produces a warm, soft white light with a natural flicker effect, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere. At 1.5 x 2.3 inches, their compact size ensures they fit effortlessly into most candle holders, making them a versatile decoration option.

Perfect for Halloween, Thanksgiving, or any festive gathering, these timer LED candles are ideal for adding a magical touch to your decor. Their flexible placement options make them perfect for bookshelves, wish bottles, gift bags, or even inside old candle shells. Great for weddings, table settings, or holiday displays, they provide a safe alternative to real candles that never needs trimming or replacing. With a modern, flameless design, these novelty lighting options offer a clean and worry-free way to enjoy warm, ambient lighting in your home or special events.
