Flameless Votive Candles with Timer and Real Wax Design

These flameless votive candles are designed to bring warmth and charm to any space without the risk of open flames. Equipped with a built-in timer, they operate automatically on a 6-hour on and 18-hour off cycle, perfect for creating a cozy ambiance without the need for manual control. Each candle is crafted from premium real wax, offering a lifelike appearance with natural drip effects and genuine flickering that mimics the look and feel of traditional candles.

With a long-lasting CR2450 battery, they provide up to 400+ hours of continuous use, making them ideal for extended events or as a daily home decor accent. The 3D wick design enhances their visual appeal, ensuring they look just as stunning in both indoor and outdoor settings. Whether you're hosting a romantic wedding, decorating for a festive holiday, or adding a touch of elegance to a themed party, these candles offer a safe and stylish alternative to real flames.
