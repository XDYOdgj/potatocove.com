NURADA Waterproof Outdoor LED Candles

Create a magical ambiance anywhere with these durable, waterproof LED candles. Designed for outdoor use, they offer a safe and worry-free alternative to traditional candles, perfect for patios, porches, and backyards. The eco-friendly wick ensures a clean burn, while the robust plastic casing stands up to all weather conditions, including heavy rain, mist, and cold.

Their impressive 8" x 4" size makes a strong visual impact, ideal for enhancing any outdoor or festive setting. Whether you're decorating for Halloween, Thanksgiving, or simply setting the mood for a cozy evening, these candles deliver a reliable glow without the risks of fire or wax.

With remote control capabilities, you can customize the lighting experience using timer settings for 2H, 4H, 6H, or 8H. Choose between flicker or steady glow modes, and adjust brightness to suit your desired atmosphere. Each candle requires 3 x AA batteries (not included) and provides up to 500 hours of continuous use, making them a great long-term investment for outdoor lighting needs.
